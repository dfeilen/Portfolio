About

Devin Feilen is a photographer based in Portland, Oregon who specializes in sports and lifestyle photography. He strives to bring a unique perspective with abstract and contextual compositions that are a step outside the "norm" of professional sports photography.


This portfolio is meant to be a unique display of work that gives the user a different experience each time it's viewed. If you are able, please view it on a screen larger than your phone to get the full experience.
